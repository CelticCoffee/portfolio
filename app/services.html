Website Design & Development

What is Web Design?

Website Design is the process of creating the look and feel of a website. Design encompasses not only decisions like colors and fonts but also the user experience for visitors to your site which includes things like menus, buttons, the organization of content and the way in which any images will be displayed.

Beyond the obvious goal of creating beautiful, informative websites, I prioritize Search Engine Optimization (SEO) in my design approach.
Some of the features I emphasize include:

  • Mobile First and Responsive Design
  • Content optimized for fast loading time
  • Information is easy to find
  • Accessibility for Screen Readers
  • Clear calls to action

I Am Both a Designer and Developer. . .Why Does That Matter?

Web Development is all about the technical side of a website. As a developer, I can engineer a functioning website without depending on existing templates or design programs. In fact, this website was built from scratch. Because I understand the whole process, I can also take existing templates, programs and plugins and modify them to fit your unique website needs.

A good example is a site I recently built in WordPress for a client. We used an existing template to save time and costs but then I modified the template so that it was very customized. One of the things we did not like about the template however was the Footer (the content at the very bottom of each page of the website). So, I was able to use my developer skills to go into the code and create a modified version of the Footer that worked harder to provide branding and SEO for her site.

You can often find some of what you want in existing code and existing templates but just as YOU and your business are totally unique, your site doesn't have to be limited by what is currently available.

Even when I'm not using all the cool Full Stack JavaScript I know to work on a site, my development training is incredibly handy for trouble-shooting websites. I know how to go about finding out what is wrong.

Finally, often external code and plugins have a lot of features beyond what you might need. That can add up to slower website speed and more vulnerability on your site from hackers. I am very pragmatic when it comes to choosing to integrate existent code when it will benefit your site and writing my own when it will translate to a safer, faster, more effective website for you.

Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

Search Engine Optimization is the process of impacting the visibility of a website in a search engine's unpaid results. Many people assume SEO just refers to paying for Google's AdWords but while paid search results and SEO have the same goal, they are actually very different.

Did you know that SEO is actually FREE?

How You Can Improve Your SEO:

  • Know and use keywords on your site that your potential clients are using in their web searches
  • Label ALL of your images with words that will be relevant to your keyword searches and/or brand name.
  • Provide regularly updated content to your website whether that is through a blog, new images etc.
  • Think carefully about your titles and subtitles on your site.
  • Watch out for hiding important text content inside of PDFs or images. While getting a PDF or JPEG with your excellent content onto Pinterest might be a reasonable tradeoff, a web crawler will not index the words inside an image even if it is embedded in your site.

I Can Help You Improve your SEO!

  • I use Google tools such as Search Console and Analytics to assess and monitor your site's performance so that when you try something, you know if it was effective!
  • I incorporate behind the scenes code like schema.org and rich snippets so that your website reads well not only for humans but also for web crawlers. I call this writing for "humans and robots."
  • Did you know that making sure your website functions well on mobile devices will automatically make it rank more highly with Google? All of my work takes this fact into account.

There are many more ways that I can assist in improving your website search rankings but I wouldn't be too effective in my business if I shared all of my secrets here would I?

Hello, world!

My name is Wendy Goodwin and I am a UI Designer and Developer in Portland, Oregon. I am greatly interested in creating technology that empowers people to create and connect with greater enjoyment and ease.

I love design but I also like the logic and the backend of the process. Writing routes and installing npm packages is fun! Right now I am excited about the amazing organizational capacity of frameworks like Backbone (okay, technically a library but you get my point). Most of all though, I like working with a team of passionate colleagues to solve new challenges.

Prior to this deep dive into coding, I ran my own music production company, Effesenden Music for thirteen years. In this role, I wrote and performed original violin music which is availabile on iTunes and Pandora and crafted customized music for high-end weddings in the Pacific Northwest, California and Hawaii. I toured with grammy nominated artists and produced my own concert series benefiting local charities. Countless hours spent calming and accommodating stressed out brides and working with artistic personalities have left me chilled out in the face of debugging and browser compatibility issues.

When I'm not coding, I love to travel with my family. Whether hiking in the North Cascades or finding the best croissant in Paris, I am fascinated with life and love experiencing new things.
